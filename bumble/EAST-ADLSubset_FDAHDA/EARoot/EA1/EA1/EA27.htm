The FunctionClientServerPort is a FunctionPort for client-server interaction. A number of FunctionClientServerPorts of clientServerType "client" can be connected to one FunctionClientServerPort of clientServerType "server", i.e. when connected the multiplicity for the connection is n to 1 for client and server.<br/><br/>Semantics: <br/>The FunctionClientServerPort is a FunctionPort for client-server interaction.<br/><br/>FunctionClientServerPorts are single buffer overwrite and nonconsumable.<br/><br/>Constraints:<br/>[1] A FunctionClientServerPort of clientServerType "client" can only be connected to one FunctionClientServerPort of clientServerType "server".<br/><br/>Extension: UML Port<br/>
