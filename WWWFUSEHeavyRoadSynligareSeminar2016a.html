Invitation

 

Expectations on vehicle systems are ever increasing, and their development face profound challenges. Safety, functionality, performance, time to market are just some of the constraints that need to be respected, in a constantly improving approach to system design and its development methods.

 

FUSE and Synligare are FFI projects addressing some of these challenges and we invite to a seminar where we want to present problem formulations and preliminary concepts and tools.

 

The two projects have complementary scope:

 

Synligare is about model based engineering methods and tools, allowing more efficient collaboration within and across organizations.

 

FUSE is about methods and architectures meeting the challenges of autonomous driving.

 

Topics:

 

  • Needs on engineering methods and architectures for autonomous driving
  • Concepts for model-based engineering of safety-related automotive systems
  • Representation
  • Model Exchange
  • Visualization
  • Metrics

 

Time: May 22nd 9-12 (Presentation), 12-14 (Tool demos)

 

Location: Volvo Group, Advanced Technology And Research, Lundbystrand, Gothenburg

 

Registration: info@synligare.eu
