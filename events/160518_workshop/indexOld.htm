Invitation

 

Automotive software and electronics contribute to much of both value and cost for next generation vehicles. For this reason, it is critical to reduce development time and risk while meeting product constraints on safety, functionality and performance.

FUSE, HeavyRoad and Synligare are FFI projects addressing some of these challenges and we invite to a seminar where we will describe engineering needs along with concepts and tools.

The three projects have complementary scope:

FUSE is about methods and architectures meeting the challenges of autonomous driving.

HeavyRoad is about virtual integration, simulation and continuous integration

Synligare is about model based engineering methods and tools with focus on collaboration within and across organizations.

Topics:

 

  • Needs on engineering methods and architectures for autonomous driving
  • Concepts for model-based engineering of safety-related automotive systems
  • Virtual Integration needs and Concepts
  • Representation
  • Model Exchange
  • Visualization
  • Simulation
  • Metrics
